  1. Heat a large skillet or dutch oven over medium high heat and add the olive oil and diced onion. Cook, stirring occasionally until the onion starts to brown and soften, about 5 minutes. Add in the salt and minced garlic and stir to combine. Cook for another 1 minute until the garlic is fragrant
  2. Add the tomato paste to the pan and stir into the garlic and onions, let cook for 2 minutes to toast the tomato paste then add in the red chili and stew meat. Allow the meat to cook undisturbed for about 3 minutes, then continue cooking, stirring occasionally for 5 minutes to brown the meat. Sprinkle the Xawaash spice blend over everything and stir to combine.
  3. Add the diced tomato, diced potato, bouillon powder and water and stir everything together. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium low and let simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. After 15 minutes cooking add in the diced green bell pepper and chopped cilantro, let simmer for another 5-10 minutes then remove from the heat and serve with flat bread or rice
